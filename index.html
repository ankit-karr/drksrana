About Me

Trusted Pediatric Care with 20+ Years of Experience

Dr. KS Rana is a senior pediatrician with over 20 years of experience in providing comprehensive child healthcare. Widely recognized as one of the best pediatricians in Cooch Behar and a trusted paediatrician in North Bengal, he is known for ethical medical practice, accurate diagnosis, and compassionate care. Dr. Rana holds prestigious qualifications including MD (Pediatrics), DNB (Pediatrics), and MRCPCH-II (UK), reflecting both national and international standards of pediatric excellence. His areas of expertise include newborn and infant care, vaccination and immunization, childhood infections, respiratory and digestive disorders, nutrition, growth monitoring, and child development. With a calm, child-friendly consultation style and a strong focus on parent education, Dr. Rana has earned the trust of generations of families across Cooch Behar and surrounding regions
